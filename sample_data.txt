Apple Inc. was founded by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak in 1976. The company headquarters is located in Cupertino, California. Tim Cook is the current CEO of Apple.

In 2022, Apple released the iPhone 14 series, which includes features like Emergency SOS via satellite. The company also introduced the new M2 chip that powers the MacBook Air and 13-inch MacBook Pro.

Apple acquired Beats Electronics in 2014 for $3 billion. Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine founded Beats in 2006. The acquisition helped Apple expand its audio product line.

OpenAI released GPT-4 in March 2023. The language model demonstrates human-level performance on various professional and academic benchmarks. Microsoft has invested billions of dollars in OpenAI and integrated GPT models into its products like Bing and Microsoft 365.

Tesla, led by CEO Elon Musk, delivered over 1.3 million electric vehicles in 2022. The company operates gigafactories in Nevada, New York, Shanghai, Berlin, and Texas. In 2022, Tesla's revenue reached $81.5 billion.
